Produktbeschreibung

ERJ 3E series automotive grade general purpose precision thick film chip resistor. This ERJ series resistor has metal glaze thick film resistive element and three layers of electrodes that make them highly reliable.
  • AEC-Q200 compliant
  • Small size and lightweight
  • Suitable for both reflow and flow soldering
